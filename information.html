THE CHEAPEST TRIP

The cheapest trip in what I have been is my trip to OSLO, NORWAY. Probably you will ask, how is it even possible not to spend a lot of money in such an expensive country? I will share some secrets with you:

  • Book tickets to Norway only from low cost Avia companies (Ryanair, Wizzair)
  • Don’t spend money on hotels. Download the app couch surfing where you can stay at people’s houses for free.
  • Bring food with you from your country.

PRAGUE FOR 0 €

Excursions from volunteers (free or for donation) Enter the name of the excursion in Google and register:

  • Free tour of Prague - 3-hour daily
    Free walking tour of the main attractions of Prague. The old city, museums and cathedrals, synagogues and bridges
  • St. Vitus Cathedral<> The most famous cathedral of the city, the vaults of which can be seen from different parts of Prague, it is so magnificent and picturesque, a real pearl of Gothic art. Free admission to the front of the cathedral fence.
  • Charles Bridge
    An old picturesque bridge in the very center of the city. Today the bridge is the heart of the city, it offers a beautiful view of the old center, the Vltava river, it is decorated with ancient sculptures, and therefore it is not surprising that it is never quiet and boring here.
  • Free to eat and sleep:
    In the old Catholic Church of St. Francis of Assisi, free food is distributed and accommodation is provided for all those in need. Budget travelers can also take advantage of this.

HOW TO BOOK CHEAP TICKETS

Remember, Air companies will always try to offer you something expensive.

  1. To search for tickets, it’s not enough just to look carefully for them, you need to understand how the airlines work. Therefore, read on the Internet information about the airlines themselves and other search engines, study the scheme for receiving money, etc.
  2. IP address tracking. It may seem ridiculous to you, but such things really count. Always get your tickets incognito.
  3. Try not to refresh the page while buying tickets.
  4. On different operating systems Mac, Android, Windows - prices will be different.
  5. When you see the inscription "Only 1 place left" - do not pay attention to this, this is one of the tricks of the airlines.
  6. The more you sit on the site, study the reservation conditions and click on the link from the link, the higher the price.
  7. The best time to search for tickets is on Tuesday, Wednesday night, 1-2 hours of the night. The price may depend on the time of day, hour, location and even on the development of your country.
